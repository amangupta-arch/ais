## `tutor_message`
Maya speaks. Renders as a chat bubble with typing dots → typewriter reveal.

```yaml
content:
  text: "Today we'll factor numbers into their primes."
  typing_ms: 600       # how long the dots show before text appears
  persona_id: nova     # optional override
```

## `mcq`
Multiple choice.

```yaml
content:
  question: "Which is the GCD of 12 and 18?"
  options: ["3", "6", "9", "12"]
  correct_index: 1
  explanation: "12 = 2²·3, 18 = 2·3² → common = 2·3 = 6"
```

## `fill_in_the_blank`
Inline input. Underscores in the template become the blank.

```yaml
content:
  prompt: "Fill in the prime factorization."
  template: "12 = ___ × ___ × 3"
  blanks: ["2", "2"]
```

## `free_text` / `reflection`
Textarea answer.

```yaml
content:
  prompt: "Why is 1 not considered a prime?"
  min_chars: 30
```

The difference: `free_text` expects a problem-solving response; `reflection` is open-ended. The eyebrow label switches between *"your turn"* and *"reflection"* in the player.

## `drag_to_reorder`
Drag chips into the right order.

```yaml
content:
  prompt: "Order these steps to solve 2x + 5 = 17."
  items: ["Subtract 5", "Divide by 2", "Get x = 6"]
  correct_order: [0, 1, 2]
```

## `tap_to_match`
Two columns; tap one from each to match.

```yaml
content:
  prompt: "Match the equation to its solution."
  left: ["x + 3 = 7", "2x = 10"]
  right: ["x = 5", "x = 4"]
  pairs: [[0, 1], [1, 0]]
```

## `exercise`
"Try this on your own" — instructions + optional skip.

```yaml
content:
  instruction: "Practice on a piece of paper, then continue."
  estimated_minutes: 3
```

## `ai_conversation`
Multi-turn chat with Maya, capped at `max_turns`.

```yaml
content:
  starter_text: "I'm going to ask you to explain primes to a 10-year-old. Ready?"
  system_prompt: "You are roleplaying a curious 10-year-old."
  goal: "Get the learner to explain primes simply."
  success_criteria: "They explain primes as numbers with exactly two factors."
  max_turns: 5
```

Posts to `/api/ai/conversation`. The starter bubble runs through the typewriter; real assistant replies render instantly.

## `media`
Image, audio, or video reference.

```yaml
content:
  kind: image           # or audio / video
  url: /img/primes.svg
  caption: "Primes up to 30."
```

## `svg_graphic`
Inline SVG with optional caption.

```yaml
content:
  svg: |
    <svg viewBox=...>...</svg>
  title: "Sieve of Eratosthenes"
  caption: "Crosses out composites."
```

## `html_animation`
Inline HTML (with light sanitisation) for short animations.

```yaml
content:
  html: |
    <div class="fade-in">...</div>
  title: "..."
```

## `checkpoint`
The closing turn of a lesson. Title + summary; "Complete lesson" CTA appears here.

```yaml
content:
  title: "Real numbers, summed up"
  summary: "Three things to take with you: primes are atoms, HCF and LCM follow from prime factorization, and root-2 is irrational."
```

Renders the summary through the typewriter; tapping anywhere skips to the end.
