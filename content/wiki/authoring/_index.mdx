Content authoring is a two-phase model:

1. **Structure** lives in seed SQL + `supabase/content/bundle-courses/*.yaml`. Bundle rows + courses + lessons (titles only) are inserted via the `scripts/load-bundle-courses.ts` loader.
2. **Turn content** lives in `lesson_turns`. It's generated by Claude through the `/yaml-generate` tool, then applied to the DB by the same route.
