AI Setu (*"setu" = bridge in Sanskrit*) is a daily AI tutor that connects a learner — child or grown-up — to the next step they actually need.

A school student in Lucknow gets help with linear equations on paper. A B.Com sophomore quizzes themselves on cost accounting on the bus. A UPSC aspirant does adaptive PYQ drills with a post-mortem after every wrong answer. A working parent listens to a 10-minute prompt-craft lesson while driving home. The shape changes; Maya — the voice tutor at the centre — stays the same.
