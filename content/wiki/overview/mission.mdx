> A bridge to whatever you want to learn next.

AI Setu is a single product that serves five very different learners with the same patient tutor and the same toolkit:

- **School students (Class 6–12)** — curriculum chapters, paper-photo grading, daily 10-minute lessons.
- **College students** — programme- and semester-aware modules.
- **Competitive exam aspirants** — UPSC, JEE, NEET, CAT, CLAT, banking, SSC, GATE.
- **Working professionals** — short skill bundles for marketers, founders, engineers, teachers.
- **The merely curious** — *"What is an LLM?"* → a custom five-minute path.

The bet: a tutor that speaks Hindi-Tamil-Bengali-and-eight-more, reads your handwriting on paper, and remembers the last thing you struggled with is more useful than another flashcard app.

## What we explicitly aren't

- A general-purpose chatbot.
- A school-LMS replacement.
- An "instant homework solver".

The closest mental model is a kind, well-prepared elder cousin who happens to know your syllabus and has all the time in the world.
