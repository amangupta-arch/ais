The catalog lives in the `plans` table. Seed values come from `supabase/seed.sql` and migration 0014.

| Plan | INR / period | USD / period | Streak unlock | Lessons / day | Sort order |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| Free | ₹0 / 30 days | $0 | 1 day | 1 | 0 |
| Basic | ₹199 / 30 days | $2.99 | 9 days | unlimited | 1 |
| Advanced | ₹499 / 60 days | $6.99 | 14 days | unlimited | 2 |
| Student | ₹99 / 30 days | $1.49 | 5 days | 1 | 3 |

## Free

> Taste the method.

Eight EN-only starter courses ("ChatGPT basics" and friends) plus the first lesson of every paid course. No card, no pressure.

## Basic

> Build the habit.

The full AI-tool literacy ladder — 43 courses on ChatGPT, Canva, Gemini, Notion AI, Suno, and 38 others. Streak freezes ×2, league access.

## Advanced

> Become the operator.

22 "mastery programmes" — Cursor, GitHub Copilot, automated workflows. Auto-includes everything in Basic. Bonus bundles unlocked. Certificate of completion.

## Student

> School, simplified.

Curriculum-aligned chapters for school + college students. Parallel track to the AI-tool ladder — a learner on Advanced does NOT automatically get Student content; they have to subscribe to both.

The Student plan is fronted by three dedicated marketing pages (`/student-plan-stt`, `/student-plan-eat`, `/student-plan-bach`) with their own price points — see [student plans](/wiki/product/student-plans).

## Access rules

See [Plan model](/wiki/architecture/plan-model). Quick reference:

| User holds | Can open content gated at |
|---|---|
| free | free |
| basic | free, basic |
| advanced | free, basic, advanced |
| student | free, student |
| advanced + student | free, basic, advanced, student |

## Where the pricing shows up

- Landing page (`app/page.tsx`) — pricing block with 3 tiers (Starter / Setu Pro / Schools)
- `/student-plan-*` — cohort-specific (One subject / All subjects)
- `/profile` — "current plan" card with all active subscriptions listed

## Future payments

Today, plans are granted manually via `INSERT INTO subscriptions`. The Cashfree integration point is `app/checkout/actions.ts` — see [Cashfree integration](/wiki/operations/cashfree-integration).
