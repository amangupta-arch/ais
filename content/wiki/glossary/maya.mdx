**Maya** is the public-facing name of the AI tutor — the voice the marketing surface sells, the entity the math-quiz feedback comes from, the name the landing page repeats throughout.

Inside the app, Maya is implemented as a layer on top of the four [personas](/wiki/product/personas):

- **Nova** — warm & patient (default)
- **Arjun** — direct & demanding
- **Riya** — chill & Hinglish
- **Sensei** — old-school discipline

A learner picks one in their profile; Maya speaks through that personality.

## Where the name appears

- Marketing copy (landing, plan pages, math-quiz UI).
- AI conversation system prompts (e.g. *"You are Maya, a math tutor for the AIS learning platform"* in `app/api/ai/math-quiz/route.ts`).
- Tutor avatar component is named `NovaAvatar` for historical reasons (predates the Maya rename) — keep until the next visual refresh.

## Voice (in the technical sense)

Maya speaks with the language-specific ElevenLabs voice IDs in `lib/audio/voices.ts`. There's no single "Maya voice" today — Hindi Maya and English Maya use different voice IDs.
