A **persona** is a tutor personality the learner chooses in `/profile`. Defined as the `Persona` type in `lib/types.ts`:

```ts
export type Persona = {
  id: "nova" | "arjun" | "riya" | "sensei";
  name: string;
  tagline: string;
  blurb: string;
  glyph: string;
  avatar_url?: string;
};
```

The learner's choice is stored in `profiles.preferred_tutor_persona` (default `nova`).

| ID | Name | Tagline | Glyph |
|---|---|---|---|
| `nova` | Nova | Warm & patient | ✦ |
| `arjun` | Arjun | Direct & demanding | ◆ |
| `riya` | Riya | Chill & Hinglish | ❁ |
| `sensei` | Sensei | Old-school discipline | ☯ |

## Maya vs persona

[Maya](/wiki/glossary/maya) is the brand-facing tutor name (one). Personas are the four sub-personalities the learner picks (multiple). They're not interchangeable in copy — say "Maya" externally, refer to "the persona" or specific names internally.

## Where persona affects behaviour

- **Avatar** rendered in the lesson player and home dashboard.
- **AI conversation system prompt** — `"You are <persona.name>, a <persona.tagline> AI tutor"` (`/api/ai/conversation/route.ts`).
- **Voice** — currently unaffected (one ElevenLabs voice per language, regardless of persona). Future: distinct voice IDs per persona.
