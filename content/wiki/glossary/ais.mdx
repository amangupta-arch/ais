**AI Setu** is the product name. *Setu* (सेतु) is the Sanskrit word for bridge — the framing for the whole product is *"a bridge to whatever you want to learn next."*

The codebase nickname is **AIS** (uppercase). It shows up in:

- The repo name (`amangupta-arch/ais`).
- The Vercel project name.
- `applicationName` in `app/layout.tsx` metadata.
- The brand wordmark uses lowercase + an italic full stop: `ai setu.`
- The `सेतु` glyph appears next to the wordmark on the landing nav, in Noto Sans Devanagari.

Domain: `myaisetu.com`.
