A **cohort** is a marketing-side audience split inside the `student` plan. Three today:

| Short key | Full name | Class range | Route |
|---|---|---|---|
| `stt` | School-to-Tenth | Class 6 вЂ“ 10 | `/student-plan-stt` |
| `eat` | Eleventh-and-Twelfth | Class 11 вЂ“ 12 | `/student-plan-eat` |
| `bach` | Bachelors | Bachelors | `/student-plan-bach` |

## Where the cohort lives in code

`lib/student-plans.ts:STUDENT_COHORTS` is the single source of truth. Each cohort has:

- `shortKey` вЂ” `stt` | `eat` | `bach`
- `route` вЂ” `/student-plan-stt` etc.
- `title`, `classRange`, `intro`, `hue`
- `whatsAlsoIncluded` вЂ” bullet list shown below the tier cards
- `plans` вЂ” the two `StudentPlan` entries (one subject / all subjects)

## Cohort vs class

A **class** (`profiles.school_class`) is the slug of an actual academic level вЂ” `10`, `bba-sem-01`, etc. A **cohort** is a UX bucket grouping related classes for pricing purposes.

A learner whose `profiles.school_class = '10'` belongs to the STT cohort but doesn't carry that label on their profile. The mapping is implicit (UI-side).
