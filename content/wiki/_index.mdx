This is the internal knowledge base for **AI Setu** (myaisetu.com) — a daily AI tutor for school students, college students, exam aspirants, working professionals, and curious learners across India.

The wiki is **admin-only**, served at `/wiki`, and edited via PRs to `content/wiki/**/*.mdx`. Every Claude session that ships a non-trivial feature is expected to update the relevant wiki page in the same PR.

## Where to start

If you're new to the codebase, read in this order:

1. **Overview** — what AI Setu is and who it's for.
2. **Architecture** — the tech stack, route map, DB schema, and the pipelines that make Maya talk and read paper.
3. **Product** — plans, languages, cohorts, personas.
4. **Authoring** — how lessons get generated (the `/yaml-generate` flow).
5. **Operations** — runbooks for the things you'll do most often.
6. **Decisions** — non-obvious choices and why they're that way.

## Conventions

Every page must carry frontmatter:

```yaml
---
```
