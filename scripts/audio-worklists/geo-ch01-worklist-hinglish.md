  > Lens 1, Origin. Yeh kahan se aaya, living world se ya non-living world se? - Biotic resources biosphere se aate hain aur unme life hoti hai: forests, fish, livestock, khud insaan. - Abiotic resources non-living cheezon se bante hain: rocks, minerals, metals. - file: `geo-ch01-c1l2-t05-k0-hinglish.mp3`  _(tutor_message)_

Kuch continuous hote hain (sooraj ko humse fark nahi padta); kuch ko wapas aane mein time lagta hai (kata hua forest, sookha aquifer). - Non-renewable resources banne mein millions of years lagte hain, minerals, fossil fuels. Ek baar khatam, toh kisi bhi human timescale pe gaye. Forest khud ko renew kar leta hai agar hum mauka dein. Renewable. Doosra tag.

### Lesson 2: the-soils-of-india

- file: `geo-ch01-c3l2-t08-k0-hinglish.mp3`  _(tutor_message)_
