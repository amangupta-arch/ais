## Arc
Water looks endless — three-quarters of the planet, endlessly recycled by
the hydrological cycle. So why will two billion people face absolute
scarcity by 2025? Because scarcity isn't only about rainfall — it's about
over-use, unequal access, and pollution. This chapter moves from the
*puzzle* of a renewable resource running out, to the great 20th-century
answer (multi-purpose dams — "temples of modern India") and its heavy
costs, to the oldest and newest answer of all: catching the rain where it
falls. Shift: from "water is free and infinite" to "water is a shared,
fragile resource we must conserve, store, and divide fairly."

## Courses & lessons (3 × 3 = 9)
**C1 — The Water Scarcity Puzzle** (`the-water-scarcity-puzzle`)
1. Renewable, Yet Running Out — hydrological cycle, renewability, the 2025 paradox.
2. Why a Wet Country Runs Dry — over-exploitation, population/food/irrigation (largest user), falling groundwater, industry & cities.
3. When Water Isn't Safe to Drink — quality scarcity/pollution; Jal Jeevan Mission, Atal Bhujal Yojana.

**C2 — Multi-Purpose River Projects** (`multi-purpose-river-projects`)
1. Dams and Their Many Uses — dam defined; multi-purpose (irrigation, power, supply, flood control, navigation, fish); Bhakra-Nangal, Hirakud; Nehru "temples of modern India"; ancient structures.
2. When Dams Do Harm — altered flow & sedimentation, aquatic fragmentation, submergence, displacement, induced quakes, floods, salinisation; Sardar Sarovar / Narmada; Damodar "river of sorrow".
3. Sharing Water Wisely — inter-state disputes (Krishna-Godavari); PMKSY (har khet ko pani, per drop more crop); integrated management as the balance.

**C3 — Harvesting the Rain** (`harvesting-the-rain`)
1. Catching Water Before It's Lost — the harvesting tradition; guls/kuls, inundation channels, khadins, johads.
2. The Tankas of the Thar — rooftop harvesting + underground tankas (Rajasthan); palar pani; first-rain rule; Indira Gandhi Canal decline.
3. Old Wisdom Working Today — Gendathur (Karnataka), Shillong (Meghalaya), Tamil Nadu compulsory law, bamboo drip irrigation (Meghalaya). Closes the bundle.
